# UZLO FOUNDATION

Version 0.1

This document establishes the philosophical foundation of UZLO.

Core principles:
- The industry is a living graph of people, companies, projects and trust.
- Every participant is a node.
- Connections matter more than profiles.
- Trust is earned through real collaboration.
- Projects are temporary, relationships endure.
- UZLO is operational infrastructure for collaboration, not just another social network.
- AI strengthens the network but does not replace human trust.
- The network is open; reputation is earned.

Founder's statement:

"Everything great is created not by isolated people, but by the connections between them."
